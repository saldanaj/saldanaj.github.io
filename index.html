This how to guide will help introduce you to the BreweryDB API.

Introduction

The main purpose of this guide is introduce you to the BreweryDB API, how to access it via client and server side, and ultimately show you a brief example on what you can do with this information. If your as big of a beer enthusiats as me (I mean who isn't) you'll have a database rich with beer information right at your fingers tips.

This guide will attempt to cover several topics while understanding you, the reader, may be a novice to web development. To the right are links to the sites which are imperative in ensuring you obtain access to the BreweryDB API.

About BreweryDB API

The BreweryDB database contains all kinds of data on beers all over the world. Are you interested in finding the closests brewery to your home (or work)? Want to know the alcohol by volume of your favorite beer? Or interested in the ingredients of your favorite beer?

Well this information can be found in the BreweryDB API

Enough talking ... let's get started!
